FAQ

Can I switch between pricing tiers at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Navigate to the billing section in your account settings. For enterprise users, do contact us.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

Yes, all paid plans come with a 14 day trial. We do not need your payment information during this period.

Is there a special price for students?

Yes! Create an account using your school's email, and contact us with proof of education for 25% off on the Professional plan.

Can I transfer my domain to/from Webify?

Yes. However, once you have transferred a domain, your domain will be ineligible for transfers for 14 days.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. However, all payments made will not be refunded if you cancel before your subscription ends.
